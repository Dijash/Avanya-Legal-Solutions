About Avanya Legal Solutions & Research

Avanya Legal Solutions & Research is a client-focused law firm in Nepal providing insightful legal strategy, research, and litigation support. We believe law is not just about rules—it's about people, justice, and clarity.

Our Vision

To be a modern legal partner built on innovation, research, and results—committed to helping individuals and organizations thrive within the framework of justice.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity: We hold ethics above everything else.
  • Transparency: Clear communication and honest guidance.
  • Precision: Every document, clause, and case detail matters.
  • Innovation: Leveraging legal tech and strategy to stay ahead.

Who We Serve

We work with startups, corporations, individuals, NGOs, and cross-border clients who need reliable legal navigation in areas such as compliance, intellectual property, dispute resolution, and documentation.

Our Legal Services

We provide comprehensive legal solutions across a range of practice areas. Our approach combines deep legal knowledge with strategic insight, ensuring your rights and interests are protected.

Corporate Law

Expert support for startups and enterprises on company formation, mergers, regulatory compliance, and business structuring.

Family Law

Compassionate handling of divorce, child custody, spousal support, and family property issues.

Criminal Defense

Vigorous defense for clients facing criminal charges—protecting rights at every stage from arrest to appeal.

Immigration & Visa

Guidance on visa applications, permits, appeals, and permanent residency under Nepalese law.

Contract Drafting

Secure and enforceable contracts—tailored to your needs in business, real estate, and employment.

Civil Litigation

Dispute resolution and representation in civil courts across contractual, property, and tort matters.
